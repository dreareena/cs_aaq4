TRAVEL NOOK is a travel website in the Philippines that aims to help the Filipino people, along with our foreign guests to find the right accommodation, with the best care suited for them. As of now, we offer three (3) different destinations for your accomodation; namely Iloilo City, Antique Province, and Taguig City. These are featured because we highly recommend going to all of these three places during your visit here in the Philippines or as you go around the country. We recommend that you go to Taguig city for sightseeing around Manila and book a good transportation. As per Iloilo City, you may take a plane to Iloilo International Airport and explore the amazing malls, peaceful lives, and blessed churches. Afterwards, come and see the place where the land meets the sea in Antique. See you there!
